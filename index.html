About

Dr. Xiaoli Lian focuses on intelligent software engineering research, with a particular emphasis on software requirements description generation and quality analysis, automated [Code] generation and modification, and ethical evaluation of large models. As a project leader, he has led one National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) youth project, one sub-project under the Ministry of Science and Technology's key R&D plan, and three university-enterprise joint projects. As a key technical contributor, she has participated in several major NSFC projects, significant initiatives by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, and other GF-related key projects.

Research Interests

  • In the field of software engineering, Dr. Lian has published over 30 [Paper]s in top conferences and journals, such as ASE, ICSE, FSE, RE, REJ, and China Science. Her work has been recognized with the ACM SIGSOFT Distinguished [Paper] Award (ASE'24, corresponding author) and the TCSE Distinguished [Paper] Award (RE'24, first author). Her research achievements have earned her the Third Prize of the GF Scientific and Technological Progress Award.

  • Currently, Dr. Lian serves as a program committee member for the top conference in requirements engineering (RE), and she is a long-term reviewer for important conferences and journals in the field of software engineering. She has also guided students to win the Second Prize in the National Finals of the 2024 National College Student Innovation and Invention Competition (AI Empowering Green Agriculture) and the First Prize in the 2024 Tsinghua-Siemens Generative AI Application Competition (Industrial Software [Code] Generation).
